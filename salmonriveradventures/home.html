Welcome to Salmon River Adventures! We are happy you are here. We work hard to provide the best experience for our customers. We use state of the art equiptment and our river guides can not be beat. We offer many different trip options. Come join us for the experience of a lifetime.

1 Day of White Water River Rafting

This option is an all day adventure for your group of friends, family or coworkers. You will spend the day with one of our certified River Guides working as a team and creating memories.

1 Day of Kayaking

This option is great for a solo or group trip. Get ready to have an unforgetable day in nature. We will guide you through the rapids safely.

3 Days of White Water River Rafting

For those of you who think one day isn't enough we have your answer. This option is a 3 day experience sure to create life long memories. Our River Guides will teach you to work together and navigate the rapids safely. Bring your friends, family, youth groups or use it as a corporate retreat.
