Target Audience

"What's the weather" is for the residents and communities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The people in these communities participate in a variety of jobs, and recreational activies. There are students, families and professionals who all need to know how to plan not only their day but their week. They need to know how to dress and whether or not to bring an umbrella.

Persona 1:

A family is planning a camping trip in the spring. They want to hike, fish, and roast marshmallows over the fire. Since the spring has unreliable weather they need to know if they should bring their camping trailer, or if they can use tents. Do they need to pack extra blankets, coats and other cold weather items? Or will they be needing hiking gear, suncreen and hammocks?

Persona 2:

A young professional is starting their new job. They want to impress their new bosses, coworkers, and potential clients. They need to know if they need to buy and wear business clothes suitable for the cold or a warm day. Would need a water resistant overcoat to protect their clothes? A way to keep their laptops dry?

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Ward breakfast. Will it be held outside? Do we need to reserve the church gym?

Scenario 2: Child's birthday party. Can we have a water fight and play in the pool, or should we plan indoor games.

Scenario 3: Driving to grandma's for Christmas. Will there be too much snow to arrive safely or should we postpone the trip.

Scenario 4: School dance festival. Can the children dance in the field, or do we need to make arrangements for the gym?

Scenario 5: A trip to the waterpark. Is it a good day to go, or will it be too cold and we need to reschedule?
